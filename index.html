Welcome To Minnow

 Minnow is an adaptive educational platform designed to make high‑quality learning accessible for all. Our mission is to bridge the equity gap in education by offering personalized learning tools, built‑in accessibility features, and multilingual support so every student—regardless of background, ability, or circumstance—can thrive.
